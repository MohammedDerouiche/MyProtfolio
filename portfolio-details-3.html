Overveiw

Tis project is a comprehensive case study conducted on Kaggle. The aim of this project is to solve various marketing and sales problems for a fictional global electronic retailer, by answering specific questions using only SQL and Python's data visualization libraries. The analysis focuses on understanding customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends to provide actionable insights for business improvement.

Analysis Scope

By leveraging the provided dataset, we seek to answer the following key questions:

  • How does customer purchasing behavior vary across different age groups, genders, and geographical locations?
    This question explores the variations in purchasing patterns among different demographic groups and regions, helping to identify which segments are most active and profitable.
  • Are there any correlations between customer demographics (age, gender) and product preferences or purchase frequency?
    Uncover any significant relationships between customer demographics and their product choices or buying frequency, providing insights into targeted marketing opportunities.
  • How does customer loyalty differ between regions, and what factors influence repeat purchases?
    This analysis will identify regional differences in customer loyalty and the key factors that drive repeat purchases, helping to refine retention strategies.
  • Can we identify distinct customer segments based on their buying patterns and preferences?
    By clustering customers based on their purchasing behavior, I aim to define distinct segments, enabling personalized marketing and better resource allocation.
  • What are the top-selling products, brands, or categories across different regions and demographics?
    This question focuses on identifying the most popular products, brands, and categories in various regions and among different demographic groups, guiding inventory and marketing strategies.
  • Are there any seasonal trends or fluctuations in product demand, and how do they impact inventory management and sales forecasting?
    Understanding seasonal demand patterns will aid in optimizing inventory levels and improving the accuracy of sales forecasts, ultimately enhancing operational efficiency.
